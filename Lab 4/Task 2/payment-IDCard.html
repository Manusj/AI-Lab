Payment

We need your payment before your appointment

You will need to show a proof of payment. Therefore we ask you to pay well in advance, in order for the payment to have arrived by the time of your appointment.

The fee of 400 SEK (per person) is for your application to be assessed by the Tax Agency. This means that you will not be refunded if your application is refused.
